Mission

"FUEL has grown rapaidly and become the gateway through which early stage and developing entreprenurial ventures gain access to valuable expertise, resources, and networks of influence in Fairfield County...In an effort to bring even more life into an already vibrant downtown area, the concept of a 'business incubator' was already endorsed and further positioned as an exciting community resource for the downtown and new center of learning for the university."

Vision

"Supporting the innovative spirit of entrepreneurs is central to the core mission of Fairfield University. We accomplish this through an innovative approach that combines student led startups with a public center for entrepreneurship and university outreach in downtown fairfield.""

How it Works

After the Fairfield Startup Competition fairfield students/alumni move on to the Summer Fellows Program with FUEL. Through this program, they will be given the resources necessary to help with their start up business.In addition, FUEL also provides support for other startup businesses outside of the Fairfield University community.

Mentors

A special thank you to our mentors for dedicating their time and for sharing their knowledge of what it really is to be an innovative member of the Fairifield community.
